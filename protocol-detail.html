Mind Non-Rx 9-Week Cycle

Brain Power

A comprehensive nootropic protocol designed to enhance cognition, boost neuroplasticity, and protect against cognitive decline. Combines BDNF-boosting peptides with nootropic botanicals, neuroprotective cannabinoids, and molecular hydrogen for optimal brain performance.

The 6 Pillars

Peptides ▼
Semax — BDNF upregulation, cognitive enhancement, neuroprotection. 300-600mcg intranasal daily.

Selank — Anxiolytic, memory consolidation, GABA modulation. 250-500mcg intranasal daily.

Dihexa — HGF/c-Met pathway, synaptogenesis, memory formation. Oral, microdose protocol.
Botanicals ▼
Lion's Mane — NGF stimulation, neurogenesis. 1000mg daily.

Bacopa Monnieri — Memory consolidation, antioxidant neuroprotection. 300mg standardised extract.

Ginkgo Biloba — Cerebral blood flow, cognitive support. 120mg EGb 761 extract.
Cannabinoids ▼
CBD (Full Spectrum) — Neuroprotection, anti-inflammatory, anxiolytic. 25-50mg sublingual daily.

CBG — Neuroprotective, neurogenesis support. 10-25mg daily.

Cannabinoid layer targets neuroinflammation and supports the endocannabinoid system's role in synaptic plasticity.
Hydrogen ▼
Molecular Hydrogen Water — Selective antioxidant, crosses blood-brain barrier, reduces oxidative stress in neural tissue. 1-2 tablets in 500ml water, 2x daily.

H2 Inhalation — Optional boost. 2% H2 gas for 30-60 minutes, 3x weekly.
Lifestyle ▼
Exercise — Zone 2 cardio 3x/week for BDNF. Resistance training 2x/week.

Sleep — 7-9 hours. Optimise deep sleep for memory consolidation and glymphatic clearance.

Meditation — 20 min/day focused attention or open monitoring for neuroplasticity.
Tracking ▼
Cognitive Tests — Weekly Stroop, N-back, and reaction time benchmarks.

Sleep Quality — Track deep sleep percentage via wearable.

Subjective Journaling — Daily focus, clarity, and memory ratings (1-10 scale).

3-Phase Timeline

PHASE 1
Foundation
Weeks 1 – 3
Establish baseline, introduce core peptides at conservative doses, begin lifestyle protocols.
  • Semax 300mcg/day (intranasal)
  • Selank 250mcg/day (intranasal)
  • Lion's Mane 500mg/day ramp-up
  • CBD 25mg/day sublingual
  • H2 water 1x daily
  • Cognitive baseline testing
PHASE 2
Activation
Weeks 4 – 6
Increase to full therapeutic doses. Introduce additional compounds and boosters.
  • Semax 600mcg/day
  • Selank 500mcg/day
  • Dihexa introduced (microdose)
  • Lion's Mane 1000mg + Bacopa 300mg
  • CBD 50mg + CBG 15mg
  • H2 water 2x daily + optional inhalation
PHASE 3
Integration
Weeks 7 – 9
Consolidate gains, taper select compounds, establish maintenance protocol.
  • Semax 300-600mcg (personalised)
  • Selank taper to 250mcg
  • Dihexa cycling (5 on / 2 off)
  • Botanical maintenance doses
  • Cannabinoid maintenance
  • Full cognitive re-assessment
