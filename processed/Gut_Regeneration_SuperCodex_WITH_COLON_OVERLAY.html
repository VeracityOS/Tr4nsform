Sub-Codex I · Phoenix Gut Broth & Barrier Repair

Purpose & Clinical Logic Назначение и клиническая логика

This sub-codex establishes the non-negotiable foundation: **seal the gut lining**, reduce inflammatory leakage, restore absorption, and stabilize stool/pain before escalation into metabolic, parasite, or regenerative layers.

If there is active GI bleeding, suspected obstruction, severe dehydration, uncontrolled vomiting, or fever with rigors: pause protocols and follow the Emergency Sub-Codex (VII).

Этот раздел — фундамент: восстановление слизистой, снижение воспалительной проницаемости и стабилизация стула/боли до перехода к более сложным уровням.

При кровотечении, подозрении на непроходимость, сильной дегидратации, неукротимой рвоте или высокой температуре — остановить протокол и следовать аварийному разделу (VII).
Duration: minimum 30 days · often continuous
Cost: Lite $50–200 · Core $300–800 · Ascension $1,200+
Phoenix Bone Broth · Full Recipe & Use Костный бульон Phoenix · рецепт и применение

Ingredients

  • 2–3kg organic chicken carcasses OR beef marrow/joint bones
  • 2 carrots, 2 celery stalks
  • 1 onion (omit if histamine-sensitive)
  • 3–5cm fresh ginger
  • 3–5cm turmeric OR 1–2 tsp powder
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • Filtered water to cover
  • Optional: bay leaf; peppercorns (omit if gastritis/reactive)

Method

  • Optional: roast bones 20–30 min at 180–200°C
  • Combine ingredients, cover with water
  • Bring to gentle boil, skim foam
  • Reduce to very low simmer 12–24 hours
  • Strain; cool; refrigerate or freeze portions
  • For pancreatic compromise: cool fully and remove fat cap

Use Pattern (Clinician-adapted)

  • 2–4 small cups daily, warm, sipped slowly
  • Flare / post-procedure days: broth may replace meals temporarily
  • Refeed rule: advance textures slowly (soup → puree → soft solids)
“Bad day” minimum viable plan: warm water + broth + rest + calm breathing.

Подробный рецепт: кости + овощи + имбирь/куркума + уксус → томить 12–24 часа на слабом огне → процедить → пить тёплым малыми порциями.

При панкреатической чувствительности: охлаждать и удалять жир.

Storage: 3–4 days refrigerated · freeze portions
Barrier Peptides & Nutrients (Clinician Layer) Пептиды и нутриенты для барьера

Peptide Families (examples used in clinics)

  • BPC-157 family: mucosal repair signaling; microcirculation support; ulcer-pattern healing.
  • KPV-type tripeptides: local inflammatory signaling reduction in epithelial environments.
  • GHK-Cu (support layer): tissue remodeling signaling; skin/gut repair adjacency in some protocols.

Barrier Nutrient Logic

  • Magnesium: motility + autonomic stability adjacency.
  • Glutamine (case-by-case): enterocyte fuel in some settings; not universal—clinician decides.

Пептиды/нутриенты для восстановления слизистой применяются только под контролем врача. Последовательность важнее агрессии.

Sub-Codex II · Metabolic & Mitochondrial Warfare (ETC)

ETC Rationale (Why Energy Comes First) Логика ETC (почему энергия — прежде всего)

Gut epithelial turnover is extremely high. When mitochondria and the electron transport chain (ETC) are impaired, barrier repair stalls and inflammation persists. This layer restores **electron flow, oxygen utilization, and redox balance** in a clinician-supervised way.

Practical rule: if the patient cannot sleep, cannot keep fluids, or has uncontrolled nausea—prioritize stabilization and symptom control before adding “performance” tools.

Слизистая кишечника быстро обновляется и зависит от митохондрий. При нарушении ETC восстановление останавливается. Этот слой — про редокс и энергию.

Default duration: 30–90 days
Cost: Lite $50–200 · Core $250–800 · Ascension $1,000+
Hydrogen Therapy (Tablets / Water) Водородная терапия (таблетки/вода)

Why clinics use it

  • Selective antioxidant behavior (hydroxyl radical modulation)
  • Mitochondrial membrane protection signaling
  • Inflammation downshift without blunt immune suppression

Integration rules (non-dosing)

  • Start low in highly reactive patients; track reflux, bloating, stool changes.
  • Separate from binders and certain meds if clinician advises.
  • On chemo days, timing is clinician-led to avoid interference with therapy intent.
Do not treat hydrogen as “harmless water” in complex cases. It is a physiological input; monitor response.

Водород — физиологический модификатор редокса. Начинать осторожно, отслеживать реактивность. Тайминг при терапии — только врач.

Cost range: $50–300 / month (device or tablets vary)
Methylene Blue (Clinical Only) Метиленовый синий (только клиника)

In specialist settings, methylene blue may be used as an alternative electron carrier within the ETC. This is **not** a consumer supplement and must be governed by a clinician experienced in drug interactions.

Governance checklist

  • Medication interaction review (especially serotonergic agents and other redox-active drugs)
  • Contraindication screening + baseline vitals
  • Conservative introduction + explicit stop rules
No self-sourcing, no self-dosing. This belongs inside regulated clinical oversight.

Метиленовый синий применяется только в медицинском контроле из-за взаимодействий и рисков. Самостоятельное использование недопустимо.

Cost: clinical pricing varies

Sub-Codex III · Parasite & Pathogen Axis (FULL)

Core Principle Основной принцип

Parasites and chronic pathogens can drain nutrients, distort immune signaling, and perpetuate gut inflammation. The goal is **bandwidth recovery** and improved terrain—not “scorched earth”.

In cancer/severe GI disease: parasite work is clinician-governed only. Timing and exit strategy matter more than force.

Паразитарная/патогенная нагрузка может поддерживать воспаление. Цель — восстановить «биологическую пропускную способность», а не агрессию любой ценой.

Default duration: 30 days (staged) · many patients 60–90 days
Cost: $200–1,500 depending on track
Protocol Sequencing (All Tracks) Последовательность (для всех треков)

Phase 0 — Assess

  • Exposure history + symptom clustering (GI, skin, sleep, cravings, anemia patterns)
  • Testing where indicated (stool PCR/microscopy; inflammatory markers)

Phase 1 — Prepare (non-negotiable)

  • Hydration + minerals
  • Bowel regularity plan (avoid constipation during kill phases)
  • Liver/lymph support (gentle)
  • Barrier support from Sub-Codex I

Phase 2 — Target

  • Choose Track A, B, or C based on risk/tolerance
  • One change at a time; track symptoms daily

Phase 3 — Bind & Exit

  • Binders are timed by clinician (avoid grabbing essential meds/nutrients)
  • Ensure elimination pathways: stool/urine/sweat as tolerated

Phase 4 — Repair

  • Microbiome rebuild (phased)
  • Mucosal repair (peptide/nutrient layers as appropriate)
  • Autonomic downshift (sleep + nervous system work)
“Kill → Bind → Repair” is the integrity triangle. Missing one corner = chaos.

Последовательность: Оценка → Подготовка → Таргетирование → Связывание/выведение → Восстановление.

Default cycle: 30 days · repeat only if tolerated

Фармакологический путь: только по назначению врача, с мониторингом и обязательным этапом восстановления.

Фитопротокол: мягче, дольше, обычно лучше переносится при реактивной слизистой. Всегда с этапом восстановления.

Hybrid is often the best “real world” model: speed when needed, softness when required, repair always.

Sub-Codex IV · Regenerative Medicine (Peptides) · Gut + Neuro + Mito

Architecture & Sequencing (Non-Negotiable) Архитектура и последовательность

This sub-codex is built as a **layered stack**, introduced slowly and measured. The clinical aim is to restore: (1) barrier integrity, (2) neuro-enteric regulation, (3) mitochondrial throughput, (4) immune orchestration — in that order.

Stop Rules (any layer)

  • Worsening bleeding, black stools, severe abdominal pain
  • Fever, rigors, confusion, dehydration escalation
  • Sudden psychiatric destabilization (especially if cannabinoids introduced)
Peptides are jurisdiction-dependent, often off-label, and supply-chain sensitive. This is a clinician governance framework, not self-prescribing instructions.

Пептидный стек вводится слоями: барьер → нейро-кишечный контур → митохондрии → иммунная координация. Только под наблюдением врача.

Default duration: 30 days per layer (minimum)
Cost: Lite $300–900 · Core $1,200–3,500 · Ascension $5,000+
Layer 1 · Gut Barrier & Tissue Repair (Selected Peptides) Слой 1 · Барьер и восстановление тканей

Primary (gut-first)

  • BPC-157 family: mucosal repair signaling, microcirculation support, ulcer-pattern healing.
  • KPV-type: local inflammatory downshift in epithelial contexts.

Adjacency (repair ecosystem)

  • TB-500 / thymosin beta-4 family (context-dependent): tissue repair signaling; used cautiously with oncology governance.
  • GHK-Cu: remodeling signals; often used as a support layer when skin/gut repair are both impaired.
Goal outcome markers: less pain, more stable stool, reduced urgency, improved tolerance to nutrition and hydration.

Слой 1: пептиды для слизистой/микроциркуляции и контроля локального воспаления. Маркеры: стабильный стул, меньше боли, лучше переносимость питания.

Default duration: 30 days before adding Layer 2
Layer 2 · Neuro–Enteric / Vagus / Motility Stabilization Слой 2 · Нейро-кишечный контур / вагус / моторика

This layer targets the gut-brain axis: nausea, motility dysregulation, threat signaling, and autonomic imbalance (sympathetic dominance or dorsal vagal collapse).

Clinic pattern (families)

  • Autonomic-modulating peptide families (used to support vagal tone / nausea regulation under supervision).
  • Sleep-stabilizing peptide families (used only when the patient’s nervous system is destabilized and insomnia blocks healing).
If the nervous system can’t downshift, the gut cannot rebuild. This layer is often the difference between “protocol works” and “protocol fails”.

Слой 2: нормализация оси кишечник–мозг, тошноты, моторики, автономной регуляции. Без снижения тревожного режима кишечник не восстанавливается.

Common clinical families (examples)

  • SS-31 / elamipretide family: mitochondrial membrane stabilization signaling (specialist use).
  • MOTS-c family: metabolic signaling and stress adaptation (specialist use).
  • Humanin-family peptides: cytoprotective signaling under stress (specialist use).
Mitochondrial tools can shift energy and appetite quickly. Introduce conservatively, track sleep, anxiety, reflux, and stool.

Слой 3: митохондриальные семейства (SS-31, MOTS-c, humanin) применяются специалистами для поддержки редокса/энергии. Вводить осторожно.

Default duration: 30–60 days, clinician-governed

Common clinic families (examples)

  • Thymosin alpha-1 (TA-1) family: immune orchestration signaling (oncology-aware governance).
  • Thymalin / thymic peptide families: immunoregulatory patterns used in some jurisdictions.

Oncology integration rules

  • Any immune-modulating layer is reviewed against chemo/immunotherapy plan.
  • Use “support & orchestrate” logic, not blunt stimulation.
  • Track labs and clinical symptoms with a defined stop policy.

Слой 4: тимические семейства (например TA-1/Thymalin) — только при онкологически грамотном надзоре и мониторинге.

Sub-Codex V · Stem Cells / Exosomes (Options + Guardrails)

The Guardrail (Cancer Context) Рамки безопасности (контекст онкологии)

Regenerative cell therapies are frontier medicine. In cancer contexts, governance must explicitly consider: infection risk, clotting risk, immune signaling shifts, and tumor microenvironment uncertainty.

This section is an options map for clinician discussion. It is not a recommendation to pursue stem cells/exosomes in active malignancy without multidisciplinary sign-off.

Клеточные терапии — передний край. При онкологии требуется междисциплинарное согласование и контроль рисков.

Default planning horizon: 30 days for workup + decision
Cost: $2,500–25,000+ depending on jurisdiction and product
Option Matrix (What Clinics Offer) Матрица опций

Autologous (self-derived)

  • Bone marrow / adipose-derived MSC workflows (regulated settings)
  • Often preferred when feasible due to donor considerations
  • Requires clean processing chain and infection control

Allogeneic (donor-derived)

  • Cord tissue / donor MSC products in governed clinics or trials
  • Requires strict donor screening and manufacturing controls
  • Explicit tumor interaction and clotting risk review

Exosomes / EVs (Investigational)

  • Quality and regulation vary widely; treat as investigational
  • Require traceability, testing, and pharmacovigilance posture
  • Often positioned as adjunct signals, not “miracle cures”

Growth Factor / PRP Layer (Adjunct)

  • Used as local/systemic adjuncts in some programs
  • Indication-specific, evidence varies; avoid hype, keep governance tight

Опции: аутологичные MSC, донорские MSC, экзосомы/EV (исследовательское), PRP/факторы роста (адъювантно). Качество и регулирование критичны.

Rule: licensed facility + traceability + informed consent + long follow-up

Sub-Codex VI · Sleep / ECS / Cannabinoids (THC & CBD) + Love Field

Clinical Logic: Sleep Architecture → Repair Biology Логика: сон → восстановление

Deep sleep is one of the strongest biological repair windows. Many clinicians link stable sleep architecture with improved recovery capacity (including nighttime hormone pulses such as growth hormone). The goal here is **safe downshift**, not intoxication.

Sleep is a protocol. The gut repairs best when the nervous system feels safe.

Глубокий сон — окно восстановления. Цель — безопасное «снижение оборотов», а не интоксикация.

Default duration: 30 days to stabilize sleep pattern
Cost: $150–1,200 (jurisdiction dependent)
Cannabinoid Protocol Pattern (Sublingual Drops · Pre-Sleep) Каннабиноиды (сублингвальные капли перед сном)

Pattern used in supervised medical programs

  • Route: sublingual drops (fast, predictable onset vs edibles)
  • Timing: commonly taken ~20 minutes before intended sleep window (clinician adjusted)
  • Objective: calm the autonomic system, reduce nausea/pain, improve sleep continuity
  • ECS framing: aim for endocannabinoid balance, not “getting high”

Why it matters for gut repair

  • Reduced nighttime sympathetic activity → improved motility patterns
  • Better sleep continuity → improved tissue repair signaling
  • Lower stress hormones → less inflammatory signaling load

Governance (must-have)

  • Micro-titration only; single-variable changes; track mood/anxiety, heart rate, reflux, dreams
  • Screen for psychosis vulnerability, severe anxiety sensitivity, and medication interactions
  • Do not combine with alcohol or other sedatives unless physician explicitly manages
Claims like “shrinks cancer” are not established medical proof. This is positioned as symptom support and sleep-architecture support within a clinician-led oncology plan.

Сублингвальные капли часто применяют за ~20 минут до сна (по решению врача) для снижения боли/тошноты и улучшения непрерывности сна. Цель — баланс ECS и безопасный дауншифт.

Default duration: 30 days stabilization, then reassess

Why it’s biological

The caregiver’s nervous system is part of the patient’s medicine.

Love Field: дыхание с длинным выдохом, мягкий контакт, фразы безопасности. Это физиология, а не «вибрации».

Биорезонанс: использовать как скан и регуляцию/снижение реактивности, а не как гарантированное лечение опухоли.

Sub-Codex VII · Emergency Gut Navigation (Red Flags + Pause Rules)

Emergency Triggers (Act Immediately) Тревожные признаки (срочно)

Go to emergency care / call your clinician urgently if:

  • Black/tarry stools, vomiting blood, or rapid worsening bleeding
  • Severe abdominal pain with distension, inability to pass stool/gas (possible obstruction)
  • Fever with rigors, confusion, fainting, very low urine output (possible sepsis/dehydration)
  • Uncontrolled vomiting, inability to keep fluids for >12–24 hours
  • Sudden chest pain, shortness of breath, new neurological deficit
In these events: stop all non-essential supplements/herbals/peptides immediately unless the primary team says otherwise.

Срочно: кровь/чёрный стул, сильная боль/вздутие и задержка стула/газов, высокая температура с ознобом, спутанность, обезвоживание, неукротимая рвота.

This section overrides all other sections
Pause Rules (When to Stop the Protocol) Когда остановить протокол

Pause immediately if:

  • New severe GI pain, worsening bleeding, severe diarrhea, or dehydration signs
  • New agitation/panic/psychosis symptoms after cannabinoids or neuro layers
  • Strong “die-off” reaction beyond agreed clinician threshold

What remains (Minimum Viable Support)

  • Hydration + electrolytes as tolerated
  • Phoenix broth (small sips) if tolerated
