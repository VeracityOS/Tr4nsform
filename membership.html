Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership at any time? ▼
Yes, all memberships are month-to-month with no lock-in contracts. You can cancel directly from your Vault settings. Your data remains yours even after cancellation.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept Bitcoin (via BTCPay Server), major credit cards, and select stablecoins (USDC, USDT). All payments are processed through privacy-preserving channels.
What happens to my vault data if I downgrade? ▼
Your data is stored locally in your sovereign vault and never on our servers. Downgrading limits features but never deletes your data. You can always export before any change.
How does the monthly restock work? ▼
Protocol tier members receive an automatic restock of their active protocol each month. You set your preferred protocol, and we ship before your current cycle ends. You can pause or swap protocols at any time.
